One of my personal favorite events that we have to offer is the Danish Cup that was held in 2002 at Skateparken.

Both Tony Hawk and Matt Hoffman got to perform for the public. Skateparken is still well known to this day where parents take their kids to use their skateboards, scooters and BMX bikes.
