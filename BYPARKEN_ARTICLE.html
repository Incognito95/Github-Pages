Place: Byparken
Time: 12. April 2018
Price: 15 EUR

Rock I Byparken is a Danish music festival which is held at Folkeparken, Roskilde. People que for three days in just to get the ticket. Every year, thousands gather in Roskilde, Denmark to attend. The preparation takes months.

Various acts from all over the world come to perform. Indian craftsmen work to produce the many traditional items on display. The week before the festival, the stalls are put up and the food is prepared, so that everything will be ready for the big day.

The festival begins at 4pm and theirs plenty of acts to see and so much green scenery to walk around and enjoy the warm summer weather. The festival is so fun and entertaining. It is a wonderful way to spend time with the family during your holiday!.
