Place: Folkeparken
Time: Thursday April - September 7-9pm
Price: Free

Last but not least is the car event, which is a great place for the elderly people to enjoy their time to look around, especially for those that love classic cars.

Every thursday night theirs a car meet up with some of the best cars all the way from all the way back before we were born.

You can experience the event every Thursday from 7pm to 9pm from April to September. Come in good time, so you can experience the spectacular sight, when many 'olides' cruise around.
